Our Story

About Nehmat Bakery

From a Kitchen Table to Your Doorstep

The name Nehmat (نعمت) means blessing and bounty — and that is exactly what we strive to bring to your table every single morning. Our bakery started as a family tradition: a warm kitchen, the scent of freshly baked bread filling the house before sunrise, and neighbours knocking on the door.

Over the years, those neighbourly requests became a small but proud home business. Today we bake traditional white and whole-wheat Taftoon flatbreads alongside pure homemade dairy products — all crafted in small batches with the same care we've always offered.

We source our flour and milk locally, keep our recipes honest, and refuse to cut corners. There are no preservatives in our bread and no shortcuts in our cream. Just real food, made by real hands.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌾

Honest Ingredients

We use only natural, locally sourced ingredients — flour, water, salt, and fresh whole milk. Nothing hidden. Nothing artificial.

🕐

Time & Patience

Good bread cannot be rushed. We give every dough the time it needs to develop flavour and achieve the perfect texture.

🏡

Home Kitchen Warmth

Our small size is our greatest strength. Every loaf, every jar of cream gets hands-on attention that a large factory never could.

📿

Persian Heritage

Taftoon bread has been a staple of Persian breakfast for centuries. We honour that legacy by preserving its authentic taste and texture.

♻️

Zero Waste

We bake to order and in small batches to avoid waste — better for you and better for the environment.

🤝

Community First

Our customers are our neighbours. We take pride in being a trusted part of the community that feeds us.
